Tokyo has been voted the most beautiful destination for this spring!

Spring in Tokyo is arguably the best time of year to visit—and it’s all thanks to hanami. This is an incredible period in the calendar to see the capital (and Japan as a whole) at its best, with cherry blossom trees blooming all over. The temperatures are also a reason to travel in Japan at this time, with the weather being quite mild (after May, the days get hotter and in summer, Tokyo can be uncomfortably humid)..
